There once was a man who refused to leave his house during a flood. When the water was two feet deep in his living room, one of his neighbors came by in his boat and offered to take him to higher ground. The man said "I'm not leaving. The water kept rising and the man was on his roof, clinging to the chimney as the water swirled around him. A police rescue boat came by to help but the man refused. "God will save me." Soon the water swept the man away and he drowned. When he got to heaven, he demanded to speak to God. "Holy Father, I put my faith in you. Why didn't you help me?" God looked down at him and screamed "YOU IDIOT! I SENT THREE BOATS!"
